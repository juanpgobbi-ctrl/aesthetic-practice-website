01

Botulinum Toxin

Treatment of dynamic wrinkles

Botulinum toxin selectively inhibits overactive facial muscles. The result is a rested, relaxed expression — without losing natural movement. Dosage is individually tailored to your muscle activity and aesthetic goals.

  • Forehead lines, frown lines (glabella), crow's feet
  • Bunny lines, lip contour, chin dimpling
  • Hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating) — armpits, hands
  • Bruxism (teeth grinding) — masseter treatment
  • Adjunctive treatment for chronic migraine
Onset3 – 7 days
Duration3 – 6 months
Session timeapprox. 30 min
DowntimeNone

02

Hyaluronic Acid Fillers

Volume restoration & contouring

Hyaluronic acid is a natural component of connective tissue. Used as a filler, it can restore volume, sharpen contours and harmonise facial proportions. Results are immediately visible and reversible with hyaluronidase if needed.

  • Lips — shape, definition, subtle volume
  • Cheeks — restoring lost volume
  • Nasolabial folds, marionette lines
  • Chin and jawline — definition and proportion
  • Non-surgical nose correction (rhinoplasty)
  • Tear trough — brightening and volume
OnsetImmediate
Duration9 – 18 months
Session timeapprox. 30 – 60 min
ReversibleYes, with hyaluronidase

03

Lipolysis

Non-surgical fat reduction

Injection lipolysis dissolves localised fat deposits by introducing active substances into the targeted area. The dissolved fat cells are broken down naturally by the body. Particularly effective for smaller, defined areas that are resistant to diet and exercise.

  • Double chin — the most common indication
  • Small, localised fat deposits
  • Permanent reduction of treated fat cells
  • Multiple sessions recommended for optimal results
  • No surgery, no general anaesthesia
Visible resultsAfter 4 – 8 weeks
Sessionsusually 2–6, depending on individual response
Session timeapprox. 30 – 45 min
ResultsPermanent

04

Biostimulators

Profhilo · Sculptra · Polynucleotides

Biostimulators activate the body's own collagen and elastin production. Unlike traditional fillers, they don't add volume — they improve skin quality from within. Results develop gradually and are long-lasting.

Profhilo

Highly concentrated, non-crosslinked hyaluronic acid that penetrates deep into the skin to stimulate cell renewal. Two sessions one month apart; results lasting up to six months.

Sculptra (PLLA)

Poly-L-lactic acid stimulates collagen synthesis over months. Gradual firming and volume restoration for advanced volume loss.

Polynucleotides

DNA fragments that promote tissue repair, improve hydration and skin structure. Particularly suitable for delicate areas such as the eye area and neck.

ResultsGradual, over weeks
Duration6 – 18 months
Sessionsusually 2–6, depending on individual response
DowntimeMinimal

Mesotherapy

Targeted active ingredient combinations

In mesotherapy, individually tailored cocktails of vitamins, amino acids, antioxidants and hyaluronic acid are introduced directly into the skin. The result is fresher, more radiant skin with improved hydration.

  • Scalp mesotherapy for hair loss
  • Individually tailored cocktail composition

Important medical information

Every aesthetic treatment requires medical indication. Prior to treatment, a thorough medical history is taken and full informed consent is obtained regarding risks, side effects and expected outcomes. A treatment will only proceed when it is medically appropriate and safe.

No guarantee can be given for a specific aesthetic result, as individual factors such as skin condition, age and metabolism influence outcomes.

In accordance with German healthcare advertising regulations (HWG), no before-and-after photographs are published on this website. Examples of similar treatments can be shown during a personal consultation.
