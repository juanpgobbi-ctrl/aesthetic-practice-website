About me

Medical expertise meets
aesthetic sensibility.

I'm Juan Gobbi — a physician trained in Buenos Aires, working in Berlin for several years. My clinical path took me through general and plastic surgery and into intensive care medicine, where I still work today.

This combination defines my approach to aesthetic medicine: a deep understanding of anatomy, safety as the highest principle, and a trained eye for what keeps a face in balance.

Since 2022, I have been offering aesthetic treatments — exclusively based on medical indication, using licensed products and in a calm, discreet environment. My focus is on injectable treatments: botulinum toxin, hyaluronic acid fillers, biostimulators and other minimally invasive procedures.

For me, aesthetic medicine is not about correcting flaws. It's about enhancing what is already there — with restraint, precision and medical intelligence.

Consultations and treatments available in German, English, Spanish and French.
