I'm Juan Gobbi — a physician trained in Buenos Aires, practicing in Berlin for several years. My background in surgery and intensive care shapes how I approach aesthetic treatments: precise, anatomically informed, and always restrained.

My goal is never an obvious result — but a harmonious one.

What I offer

Wrinkle Treatment

Targeted relaxation of overactive facial muscles. Forehead lines, frown lines, crow's feet — a rested expression without losing natural movement.

Bruxism Treatment

Relaxation of the masseter muscle for teeth grinding and jaw pain — therapeutically effective and optionally slimming the facial oval.

Migraine Treatment

Therapeutic use for the prevention of chronic migraine attacks. Scientifically recognised, individually dosed.

Lip Volume

Subtle volume enhancement and contouring of the lips with hyaluronic acid. Natural results that suit your features.

Non-Surgical Nose Correction

Smoothing irregularities and lifting the tip with hyaluronic acid — minimally invasive, no downtime.

Fat Dissolving Injection

Non-surgical reduction of localised fat deposits such as double chin. Permanent results without surgery.

Skin Rejuvenation

Biostimulators stimulate the body's own collagen production. Firmer, more elastic skin — from within, without changing the face.

Skin Booster

Individual combinations of vitamins and hyaluronic acid. Brighter, deeply hydrated skin.

Salmon Sperm Treatment

PDRN — polynucleotides derived from salmon DNA — stimulate deep tissue repair, improve hydration and restore skin density. Ideal for delicate areas such as the eye contour and neck.

How does my first appointment work?

Every treatment starts with a detailed consultation. Only after discussing options and establishing medical indication will I proceed — never the other way around.

Will the results look natural?

That is always the goal. No exaggerated volume, no frozen expression — an improvement you feel without it being immediately obvious.

Who are these treatments for?

For adults seeking targeted, medically supervised aesthetic treatment. In the consultation we'll discuss together what makes sense for you.
