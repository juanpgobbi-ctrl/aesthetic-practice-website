Preparation

What you should know before your treatment.

Good preparation is part of a good treatment. Here you'll find all the key information to help you arrive at your appointment as well-prepared as possible.

General advice

  • Come to your appointment without make-up or lightly made up
  • Drink plenty of water on the day of your appointment
  • Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before treatment
  • Inform me of all current medications
  • Let me know about any known allergies in advance

Blood thinners & medication

  • Avoid ibuprofen and aspirin for 3–5 days beforehand if possible
  • Pause omega-3 fatty acids and fish oil for 1 week before
  • High-dose vitamin E supplements should also be paused
  • Do not stop prescription blood thinners without medical advice
  • When in doubt: discuss before your appointment

Contraindications

  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding
  • Active skin infections in the treatment area
  • Acute illness (fever, infection)
  • Known allergy to components of the products used
  • Certain autoimmune conditions — please discuss beforehand

After treatment

  • No exercise on the day of treatment
  • Avoid sauna, sunbed and direct sun exposure for 48 h
  • Do not rub or massage the treated area (4 h for botox)
  • Stay upright for 4 hours after botox treatment
  • Mild swelling and bruising are normal and will resolve

These are general guidelines. Depending on the treatment and your individual situation, there may be specific recommendations. We'll discuss everything in detail during your consultation.
